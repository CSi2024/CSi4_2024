AI-Infused Pedagogies for Fostering Critical Thinking


Mr. Chuah Kee Man

This workshop explores the transformative potential of artificial intelligence (AI) in enhancing educational methodologies aimed at developing critical thinking skills. This approach integrates AI technologies into teaching strategies. Participants will discover how AI can create interactive and engaging learning environments that can foster students' abilities to analyse, evaluate, and synthesize information critically. Through practical examples and case studies, the workshop will demonstrate how AI can promote active learning and prepare students for complex problem-solving to thrive in the digital age.
AI Authors: Ethical Considerations in Using ChatGPT for Academic Writing


Assoc. Prof. Dr. Ummi Naiemah Saraih

Join us for an insightful workshop titled "AI Authors: Ethical Considerations in Using ChatGPT for Academic Writing," where we will explore the transformative role of AI in academic writing, with a focus on ethical guidelines. We will cover the capabilities and examples of ChatGPT in drafting academic content, address intellectual property and authorship concerns, discuss plagiarism and originality challenges, and outline best practices for ethical AI use in research. This workshop will equip you with the knowledge and tools to integrate ChatGPT into your academic writing responsibly and effectively.
Leveraging AI in Research Writing Publication


Dr. Arfan Fahmi

The workshop involves utilizing AI tools and techniques to enhance various aspects of the research process. They can be applied in each research writing steps. AI technologies have the potential to streamline the research writing and publication process. However, careful integration and validation are necessary to ensure the integrity and ethical standards of research publication are maintained.
Basic Digital Photography and Mobilegraphy


Mr. Zuraidi Ahmad Mokhdzar

In this workshop, participants learn the basic principles of the photographic media, basic camera functions, become familiar with various settings and presets on camera, explore the photographic process from pre-visualization, taking images, digital storage media and transfer of images, to learning design principles, composition, and fundamental history and theory of photographic media.
Creative AI: Transforming Multimedia for Engaging Content


YM Tengku Kastriafuddin Shah Tengku Yaakob

Discover how AI can revolutionize your multimedia presentations in this workshop. Learn the tips and tricks to create captivating slides, posters, and videos using advanced AI tools. Enhance your design skills, streamline your workflow, and produce creative content that captivates and engages your audience. Perfect for educators and professionals seeking to elevate their presentation game with cutting-edge technology.








An Introduction to AI and Machine Learning Implementation


Dr. Mohd Shuhanaz Zanar Azalan

Workshop on Basic Deep Learning (DL) Applications will guide participants in the practical aspects of designing and implementing DL solutions for real-world problems. Participants began by exploring the implementation of DL models in open-source platforms (Python). Using frameworks like TensorFlow and PyTorch, they learned to build neural networks for solving complex tasks. Practical sessions covered setting up environments, coding models, and optimizing performance metrics such as accuracy and computational efficiency. The workshop equipped participants with essential skills in implementing DL in open-source platforms, designing applications for specific problems. By integrating theory with practical application, participant is prepared to tackle complex challenges in the field of machine learning and contribute to innovative solutions in industry and research.
Smart Referencing using Mendeley


Assoc. Prof. Ts. Dr. Wan Zuki Azman Wan Muhamad

This workshop is designed to equip researchers, students, and academics with the skills to efficiently manage and organize their references using Mendeley. Participants will learn how to import, organize, and annotate references, as well as how to seamlessly integrate citations and bibliographies into their documents. The workshop will also cover advanced features of Mendeley, including collaborative tools and research data management, ensuring attendees leave with a comprehensive understanding of how to leverage Mendeley for smarter referencing and more effective research workflows.
Press Releases for Digital Media


Dr. Ribut Priadi., S.Sos., M.Ikom.

Join us for an insightful workshop titled "Press Releases in Digital Media”. Economic liberalization demands universities innovate to attract public interest. Effective public relations (PR) are vital for shaping a university's image and building trust. In the digital age, PR professionals must skillfully use social media and write engaging press releases to enhance reputations and manage crises.
